The dataset contains information about students' performance, including their gender, race/ethnicity, parental level of education, type of lunch (standard or free/reduced), whether they completed a test preparation course, and their scores in math, reading, and writing.

Gender: Indicates the gender of the student (male or female).
Race/Ethnicity: Represents the racial or ethnic group to which the student belongs, categorized into groups A, B, C, D, or E.
Parental Level of Education: Describes the highest level of education attained by the student's parents or guardians, ranging from some high school education to master's degree.
Lunch: Specifies the type of lunch provided to the student, categorized as either standard or free/reduced.
Test Preparation Course: Indicates whether the student completed a test preparation course (none or completed).
Math Score: The score obtained by the student in the math exam.
Reading Score: The score obtained by the student in the reading exam.
Writing Score: The score obtained by the student in the writing exam.
These variables provide insights into the academic performance of students and potential factors that may influence their scores, such as socio-economic background, parental education, and test preparation. Analysis of this metadata can help in understanding patterns and relationships within the dataset and identifying areas for further investigation or intervention to support student achievement.
